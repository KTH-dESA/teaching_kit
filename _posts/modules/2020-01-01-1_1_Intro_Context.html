--- #Energy policy
Energy policy is the manner in which a given entity has decided to address issues of energy planning including energy supply, distribution and end-use.



It includes aspects related to:

Energy security

Environmental protection

Market structures

Incentives or disincentives (e.g., FITs, carbon taxes)

Directives (e.g. measure for efficiency improvements)
